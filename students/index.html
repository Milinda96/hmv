Students who left special memories of School

K.M.Kusum Kumari

  • K.M.Kusum Kumari was the first student to enter the government service (teaching profession) after continuing his education from grade 1 to grade 13 at this school. (In the year 2014)

M.P.Sachini Dilhani

  • Obtaining an external degree for the first time after continuing education from grade 1 to grade 13 at this school.

J.P.Duleesha Madhurenu

  • Obtaining an external degree for the first time after continuing education from grade 1 to grade 13 at this school.

G.W.Chanaka Nuwan Kumara

  • G.C.Chanaka Nuwan Kumara became the first student (in the GCE (A / L) examination 2011) to be selected for a university after continuing his education from grade 1 to grade 13 in this school.

G.R.A.Asela Madhuwantha

  • G.R.A.Asela Madhuwantha was the first student to give this school first place in a subject. (1st place in Grade 8 in the 201 – 500 student category of the Sociology Competition held in 2013)

W.M.Chanaka Madhushanka

  • W.M.Chanaka Madushanka won the first All Island “Gold Medal” for this school in sports. (From the boys under 15 javelin throw event in 2013)
