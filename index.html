HEEWELKANDURA MAHA VIDYALAYA BADULLA

Today there are about 800 students are studying at B/ Heewelkandura Maha Vidyalaya under the guidance of 15 teachers. At present, the principal of B/ Heewelkandura Maha Vidyalaya is Mr.D.G.R.Mangala Roopasinghe. The school provides many facilities to improve the knowledge of the students. The Information and Communication Technology Unit, Physical Education Unit, Library Unit, Commerce Unit, Art Unit are some of them. Under the guidance of the present Principal Mr.D.G.R.Mangala Roopasinghe, the school twinkles as the best school in Uva Province, and also the obedience of students upraise the glory of the school. In the future, the school will be developed for the next generations and will be a leading school in Sri Lanka.
